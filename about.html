OUR ADVANTAGES

01.Excellent Quality

Every product manufactured at the state-of-the-art unit of Dhanjal Engineering Works is synonymous with perfect quality and design. High standards of quality are maintained with full vigor to make sure that customers are offered only premium grade products. A stringent quality management system is followed at every stage, be it in raw material procurement, product manufacturing, product packaging or product dispatch, etc. From start to the end, every stage is under the careful purview of our expert quality control inspectors, who maintain rigid quality control measures, thus, making sure that only quality products reach our clients.

02.Our Expert Team

At Dhanjal Engineering Works, team effort is given prominence as we understand the advantages that are brought forth by a united and committed team. We are well supported by a team of proficient designers, artisans, R&D experts, among others. Their coordinated efforts in providing customers with products that are in accordance with their requirements have reaped benefits for us in the form of a large base of clients. They are always willing to learn and carefully take into account the suggestions or feedback provided by our customers, and are always eager to better themselves time and again.

03.Our Hallmarks

Large variety of superlative kitchenware, flatware, bar accessories under one roof Advanced manufacturing unit, outfitted with latest and sophisticated machines Upgradation of the machines as per latest technology A properly defined network of distribution for ensuring efficient distribution of products On-time delivery and client satisfaction: Our leading edge Innovation in quality: A regular norm at our concern A spacious warehouse to store bulk products safely.

Dhanjal Engineering Works is one of the leading manufacturers and exporters.

Dhanjal Engineering Works is one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of kitchen utensils, stainless steel utensils, stainless steel kitchen utensils, designer kitchen utensils, stainless steel colanders, mixing bowls, fruit and bread basket, table ware, bar accessories, kitchen ware, etc. We have the one of the most premium quality Bar Accessories, Cutlery, House Wares, Pet Wares and Hotel Wares. Our range of products has been widely appreciated by the world wide customers for its unmatched quality and excellent finish.

With a vision to fully live up to the expectations of customers in the realm of kitchenware and allied products, Dhanjal Engineering Works has carved a deep niche in the domestic kitchenware segment. Incepted in 2006, the company has achieved immense reputation for being time conscious as well as quality conscious in its approach. The noted company is a manufacturer and supplier of premium grade bar tool sets (4 pc., 5 pc.), water jugs, service trays, bud vases, round coasters, stainless steel bottle holders, stainless steel wine coolers, stainless steel canisters, stainless steel bowls, stainless steel flatware, and many more.

Our products, available in a number of colors and designs, are ideally suitable for both commercial and domestic applications. Our committed approach to quality, timely delivery and competitive prices has been the main factor behind our success within a relatively short span of time. We leave no room for customer grievances, for we understand their worth and are always in the forefront to understand their requirements and cater to them as efficiently as possible.
